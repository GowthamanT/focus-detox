Focus Detox

created at : August 16, 2021 by Gowthaman T.

I can't be able to focus on the things I need to do. I'm a graduate who is entering into the IT Industry as a Developer. And I'm a freelancer and a person who is living his normal life like any 21 year old do.

Getting up from bed, been on mobile all over the day and doing same routines all over the week. But my dream is not this. I want to be Financially Independent. I want to be an Entrepreneur and as a web series fan I would like to quote I want to be like Mike Ross or Harvey specter from Suits or Bobby Axelrod from Billions. But what I'm actually doing is getting me nothing like them.

I tried to focus and do things that matter and do stuffs that matter. But what I lack is action. Because every day as I recall my productive time is of average an hour or two. On other times I always spending time unproductive or thinking about random things. Or searching for motivation and as I get that motivation I again search for more. I don't execute things actually.

Why am I doing this?

My mind never sits of for the things I want. It always feels comfortable when watching any motivation videos or any videos that tells somethings or web series or movies from Netflix. I tried to do stuffs that matters but I actually never done it fully. I have been on the same cycle for long.

So, Now I have decided to do some Dopamine detox and tend to get my focus on things that really matter.

My Plans for Detox.

  • Using mobile phone for only a hour in a day.

    Only using mobile phones from 8.00 PM to 9.00 PM. Only answer calls in the mean time. No social media, YouTube, Netflix or any other apps.

  • Checking mails in computer in the morning.

    Not in mobile phones, checking in pc doesn't distract me or anything.

  • Changing Sleep Cycle.

    For a long time, my sleep cycle is of 12 or 2 AM to 8 or 10AM. I am planning to change to 10PM to 6.30 AM.

  • Do some Exercises.

    I can't go to gym as we have covid restriction. I planned to go for a walk in the Morning.

Other than that doing the daily planned tasks or work tasks.

What do I want to Achieve?

I strongly believe in the quote that "Mind is the ultimate tool that humans possess". So I want to Improve myself and bring back the Focus. I want to achieve good Mental Stability.

Progress Tracking

I'm going to post tweets daily about the day and the things I'm encountering and my mind thoughts. I'm planning on doing this for first 50 days and see my progress and continues according to it or change my strategies according to my results. I'm naming this as #50daysoffocusdetox. You can see my results on my Twitter handle @gowthaman_gt.

If you want to look more on Digital Detox you can see this Medium Blog.

Takeaway

I'm Doing this for improving myself and if you too having difficulty to focus, try this and tell me what you achieve. We can speak strategies and grow together.
